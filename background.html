Curious and determined, I place great importance on continuous learning and the ongoing improvement of my skills. Professional Experience

Junior Actuarial Consultant

I joined EY’s actuarial department as a Junior Consultant, where I contributed to regulatory audit engagements and the automation of actuarial processes. This experience allowed me to strengthen my technical skills in both life and non-life insurance, as well as in programming. I developed strong proactivity and the ability to work efficiently in demanding environments under tight deadlines.

The assignments notably included:

  • Development of an actuarial artificial intelligence tool
    • Task automation, process optimization, and improved calculation reliability
    • Python programming: development and automation of the tool, integration of AI models, and creation of an interactive interface using Streamlit
  • Internal project: Development of an asset projection model within an ALM framework
    • Migration of an Excel tool to a Python environment
    • Contribution to the analytics pipeline: data preparation, business rules, and documentation
  • Actuarial review of technical provisions in P&C insurance
    • Triangle analysis, review of selected methodologies, and data quality checks for reporting purposes
  • Participation in actuarial audit assignments for insurance companies (Pro BTP, LRA)
    • Data analysis, review of assumptions, and consistency checks of results

Actuarial Apprentice

I worked as an actuarial apprentice within the Actuaries & Experts division of the Fraeris Group. My main role was to support the consultants in carrying out their assignments, contributing whenever possible. I also participated in various internal projects, such as tool development and research or writing notes on current topics.

The assignments notably included:

  • Actuarial analysis of healthcare open data
    • Handling large datasets, development of indicators, statistical modelling, and pricing analysis
  • Retirement data analysis (French Polynesia)
    • Segmentation of policyholders across the 5 archipelagos, statistical analysis of large datasets, and development of a dashboard to track pension trends
  • Technical monitoring of insurance products
    • Profitability analysis, pricing review, and development of performance indicators
  • Support in the management of pension schemes
  • Organization and execution of inventory processes
    • Provision calculations, result analysis, and audit support
  • Three-month assignment at GGVie: support in the development of new insurance products
  • Support in Solvency II-related work
    • Actuarial function and risk management

Actuarial Intern with a focus on Data Engineering
